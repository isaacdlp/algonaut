ABOUT

Alternative Insights

PEACE ON MIND AND DATA IN YOUR SCREEN

Alternative Insights provides the complete aerial intelligence platform for commodities industry.

Our mission is to help clients reach operational excellence, reduce costs and risks through technological innovation. Our company uses industry-leading operational expertise and experience to develop software and technology solutions that allow clients to effectively use the power of drone-driven data collection and analysis.

OUR ONLY TARGET: YOUR SAFETY

Alternative Insights enables clients to carry out safe and cost effective inspections worldwide in variety of sectors, including but not limited to the following industries:

  • Oil and Gas
  • Renewables
  • Metals & Mining
  • Agriculture

Our team combines decades of experience developing successful projects in geospatial service, digital orthophotography, GIS mapping, and applied machine learning-based image processing.

PROCESS

.01

GATHER

We identify the best means to gather meaningful data from the field; measuring the physical characteristics of areas of interest in 3D with a predetermined frequency so we can go beyond spot checks and create valuable time series information.

.02

STRUCTURE

We cleanse, structure and turn raw data into structured information. We provide the resources required for Big Data curating, processing and storage. We check the data sets and cross-reference them in order to identify relevant patterns.

.03

ANALYZE

We use machine learning and algorithmic methods to turn data into meaningful insights. Our data scientists understand how to separate the signal from noise and build useful predictive models to reduce risks and leverage new business oportunities.
